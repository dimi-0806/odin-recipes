Description

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork spare ribs
  • 1 bunch of kangkong or water spinach, leaves separated and stem cut into 3-inch lengths
  • 1 onion, peeled and quartered
  • 2 large tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 egg plant, ends trimmed and sliced to 1/2-inch thick rounds
  • 1 radish, peeled and sliced to 1/2-inch thick rounds
  • 1/2 bunch string beans, ends trimmed and cut into 3-inch lengths
  • 2 long green chilis
  • 8 cups water
  • fish sauce to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
