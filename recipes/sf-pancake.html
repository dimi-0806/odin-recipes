Seafood Pancake

“Haemul-pajeon 3” by bryan... is licensed under CC BY-SA 2.0.

Description

Seafood and pancakes on their own are great. They're my favorites. But the two of them in a single dish? Say no more! Seafood pancake is a crispy, savory Korean-inspired dish filled with shrimp, squid (or any seafood of your choice), and veggies in a light, golden batter. You can share it with your friends or your family, but honestly, I'd rather devour the whole thing myself :).

For a step-by-step guide on how to clean and prepare the seafood, check out Maangchi's Seafood Pancake video, where she shows you exactly how it's done. This dish is also based on her recipe, so be sure to give it a watch.

Ingredients

  • peeled and deveined shrimp, chopped
  • cleaned squid, chopped
  • 2-3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped carrots
  • 1 green chili pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup potato starch
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • vegetable oil (or any neutral oil)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix all the ingredients except for the vegetable oil. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat. Pour in about 2-3 tablespoons of vegetable oil in the pan and swirl it around to coat the pan evenly.
  3. Once the oil is hot, pour your seafood pancake mixture in the pan and spread it evenly.
  4. Fry until the bottom of the pancake is crispy or light golden brown.
  5. Flip the pancake over. Add vegetable oil around the edges of the pancake and fry until the other side is crispy or light golden brown.
  6. Transfer the cooked pancake to a plate and serve with rice or as a snack.
